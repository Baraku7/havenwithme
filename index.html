Why it matters
Every day your nervous system absorbs stress — from work, relationships, and the relentless pace of modern life. Over time this keeps your body locked in fight-or-flight, flooding you with cortisol and stealing your sleep.

Meditation and breathwork are not luxuries. They are medicine.

Just a few minutes of intentional breathing each day can physically rewire your nervous system — shifting you from anxiety into calm, from scattered into focused.

Our Privacy Promise to You
We will never sell your personal data. We will never sell your mental health information. We will never sell your conversations. Not now, not ever — regardless of any change in ownership, business circumstances, or financial pressure.
Haven was built on the belief that mental wellness support should be a safe, judgment-free experience. We understand that when you share your feelings, fears, and personal struggles with Haven, you are placing enormous trust in us. We take that trust seriously.
This Privacy Policy explains in plain, honest language exactly what information we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and the choices you have over your own data. If anything in this policy is unclear, please reach out to us at privacy@havenwithme.com — we are real people who will respond.
This policy applies to all Haven services including HavenWithMe.com, our mobile applications, and any related products or services.
Section 02
Information We Collect
We collect the minimum amount of information necessary to provide you with a meaningful, personalized experience. Here is a complete and transparent breakdown:
📋 Information You Provide Directly
  • Account Information: Your first name, last name, and email address when you create an account
  • Optional Profile Information: Phone number, date of birth, and profile photo if you choose to provide them
  • Payment Information: We use Stripe to process payments. We never see or store your full credit card number — only the last 4 digits and expiration date for your reference
  • Communications: If you contact our support team, we retain those communications to help resolve your issue
📊 Information We Collect Automatically
  • Usage Data: How often you use Haven, which features you use, and general patterns of engagement
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, browser type, and screen size — used to ensure Haven works properly on your device
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages visited, and crash reports for security and performance purposes
  • Mood Check-ins: The mood selections you make in the app (stored locally on your device)
💬 Conversation & Journal Data
  • Your chat conversations with Haven's companion are processed to generate responses
  • Journal entries are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud backup
  • We do not store the full text of your conversations permanently on our servers
Section 03

Every piece of information we collect is used for one or more of the following legitimate purposes:
  • Providing the Service: Your name and email are used to create and manage your account and personalize your Haven experience
  • Processing Payments: Subscription and billing management through our secure payment processor Stripe

  • Legal Compliance: Where required by law, we may use your information to comply with legal obligations
  • Communications: With your consent, we may send you wellness tips, product updates, and service announcements
We will never use your mental health conversations or journal entries to target you with advertisements. Your innermost thoughts are not marketing data.

We want to be crystal clear about what we do not do:

Section 05
How We Protect Your Data
We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction:
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and Haven's servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 (the same standard used by banks)
  • Encryption at Rest: Stored data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Secure Payment Processing: All payment information is handled by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security
  • Access Controls: Only authorized Haven team members with a legitimate need can access user data, and all access is logged and audited
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security reviews and penetration testing to identify and address vulnerabilities
  • Incident Response: We have a documented plan to respond quickly to any potential security incident and notify affected users promptly
While we implement robust security measures, no system is completely impenetrable. In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by applicable law.
Section 06
Who We Share Data With
We share your data with an extremely limited number of trusted service providers who help us operate Haven. Every partner is contractually bound to use your data only for the specific purpose of providing their service to Haven:
  • Stripe: Payment processing — they handle subscription billing securely. Stripe's Privacy Policy applies to payment data.
  • Anthropic: Powers our chat companion responses. Conversation data is processed to generate your response but is not retained for training purposes under our agreement.
  • Cloudflare: Network security and content delivery. They see encrypted traffic but cannot read your content.
  • EmailJS: Used only to send account-related transactional emails such as signup confirmations.
That is the complete list. We do not share your data with social media companies, advertisers, data brokers, insurance companies, employers, government agencies (unless legally required), or any other third parties.
Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if we are legally required to do so by a valid court order, subpoena, or government request. We will notify you of such requests to the extent permitted by law and will challenge requests we believe are overbroad or unlawful.
Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of Haven, your data may be transferred. We will provide advance notice and you will have the right to delete your account before any transfer occurs.
Section 07
Your Conversations & Journal — Special Protections
We recognize that your conversations with Haven and your journal entries represent your most personal and vulnerable thoughts. These deserve extraordinary protection.
Your journal entries are stored locally on your device. They are not sent to Haven's servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync in your settings. Only you can read your journal.
Conversation Processing: When you send a message to Haven's chat companion, that message is sent to our secure servers and processed to generate a response. We do not build permanent records of your full conversation history on our servers. Conversations are processed in the moment and are not retained beyond what is necessary to maintain context within your current session.
No Conversation Profiling: We do not analyze your conversations to build a psychological profile of you, target you with advertisements, or sell insights derived from your mental health discussions to any third party.
Crisis Situations: In very rare circumstances where we have credible reason to believe a user's life is in immediate danger, we may take action to provide emergency resources. We do not proactively monitor conversations but our system is designed to recognize distress signals and suggest appropriate support.
